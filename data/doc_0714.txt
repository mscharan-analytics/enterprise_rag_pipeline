STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-2714
Section: Human Resources and Operations
Subject: Corporate Travel Expense Limits
Effective Date: 2026-03-17

Purpose:   This document outlines the policy for Corporate Travel Expense Limits at Enterprise Corp.   

Guidelines:
- Employees must request approval from HR Business Partner at least 8 days in advance.
- Maximum limit per calendar year is $500 per incident.
- Any exceptions must be approved in writing by the VP of Human Resources.
Violations of this policy will lead to disciplinary action.
