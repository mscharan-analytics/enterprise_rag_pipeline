STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-2906
Section: Human Resources and Operations
Subject: Information Security Guidelines
Effective Date: 2026-07-11

Purpose:   This document outlines the policy for Information Security Guidelines at Enterprise Corp.   

Guidelines:
- Employees must request approval from Department Head at least 23 days in advance.
- Maximum limit per calendar year is $500 per incident.
- Any exceptions must be approved in writing by the VP of Human Resources.
Violations of this policy will lead to disciplinary action.
