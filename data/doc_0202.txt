STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-2202
Section: Human Resources and Operations
Subject: Employee Anti-Harassment
Effective Date: 2026-06-16

Purpose:   This document outlines the policy for Employee Anti-Harassment at Enterprise Corp.   

Guidelines:
- Employees must request approval from Direct Manager at least 11 days in advance.
- Maximum limit per calendar year is 12 weeks per birth/adoption.
- Any exceptions must be approved in writing by the VP of Human Resources.
Violations of this policy will lead to disciplinary action.
