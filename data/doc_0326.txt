STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: SOP-2326
Section: Human Resources and Operations
Subject: Parental Leave Policy
Effective Date: 2026-05-25

Purpose:   This document outlines the policy for Parental Leave Policy at Enterprise Corp.   

Guidelines:
- Employees must request approval from Department Head at least 18 days in advance.
- Maximum limit per calendar year is $500 per incident.
- Any exceptions must be approved in writing by the VP of Human Resources.
Violations of this policy will lead to disciplinary action.
